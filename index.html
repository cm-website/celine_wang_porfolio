Meet Celine Wang

Celine Wang is a passionate high school student and writer dedicated to making classical education accessible to all. As the founder of Aretas—named after the Ancient Greek term for 'excellence'—she has created a web-based platform that delivers top-notch education in Roman and Greek classics at no cost. Through her emphasis on Accessibility, Community, and Engagement (A.C.E.), Aretas provides fun yet scholarly content designed to spark curiosity in younger learners, accessible on any device without limitations.

A student journalist for The Chronicle, Celine's writing explores contemporary issues ranging from educational policy and economic trends to campus culture. Her work combines thoughtful analysis with engaging storytelling, tackling topics such as AP curriculum changes, tariff policies, and emerging social phenomena.

Beyond journalism, Celine is an accomplished young researcher with a focus on political philosophy and democracy, as well as a poet who expresses her creative vision through verse. Her diverse talents and commitment to learning reflect a deep belief in the transformative power of knowledge and the written word.
